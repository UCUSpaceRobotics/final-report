The rover's robotic hand is a 6-DOF articulated manipulator with a maximum
reach of \qty{132}{\centi\meter} and a maximum payload of
\qty{3}{\kilo\gram}, built as an anthropomorphic RRR arm with a spherical
wrist. It performs deep and surface sample collection, bioscience probe
handling, and equipment maintenance through a tool-less quick-change
end-effector interface. The manipulator in action across these tasks is
shown in \autoref{fig:manipulator_scenarios}.
