The rover electronics are organized into independent subsystems for power, communication, navigation, and robotic arm control (\autoref{fig:rover_architecture}). The modular design uses dedicated PCBs and standardized XT, JST, and terminal block connectors to simplify assembly, servicing, and module replacement. CAN serves as the primary communication interface, while USB is used for high-bandwidth peripherals.
