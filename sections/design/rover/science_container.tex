% Draft content for the science container subsystem. The structure reflects the
% current design update: two side-by-side containers, no lids, and a compact
% form factor intended to simplify handling and improve rover mobility.
% =============================================================================

The science container consists of two side-by-side compartments mounted on the
front chassis. One compartment stores rock specimens, while the other stores
surface and deep samples in two internal half-cells. The previous closing lids
were removed to simplify the structure and avoid interference with the robotic
arm during deployment. The design is kept compact, with the overall height
reduced to approximately \qty{100}{\milli\meter}, improving mobility for both
the arm and the central camera. Both compartments are equipped with load cells
and provide mass measurement with the same resolution of $\pm\,$\qty{1}{\gram}.
Each sample is weighed individually: after placement, its mass is recorded, the
system is tared, and the next sample is measured in the same manner. The
configuration is shown in \autoref{fig:science_container_scheme}, and its use is
illustrated in \autoref{fig:science_container_action}.
