The rover can be equipped with one or two 48~V, 12~Ah Li-ion battery packs, depending on the mission requirements. The power budget was estimated from measured current consumption in the main operating modes and a representative 60-minute mission profile. The estimated operating times for both battery configurations are summarized in \autoref{tab:power_budget}.
