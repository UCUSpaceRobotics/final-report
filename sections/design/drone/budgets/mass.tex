The mass budget in \autoref{tab:drone_mass_budget} categorizes the Drone
into dry mass, battery, and payload, with a lightweight frame and core
avionics keeping baseline mass low. A \qty{15}{\percent} engineering margin
is applied to the system subtotal to account for unmodeled items such as
wiring, fasteners, and conformal coating, resulting in a calculated
Maximum Take-Off Mass (MTOM) of \qty{2.75}{\kilo\gram}, which remains well
below the \qty{5.0}{\kilo\gram} limit for the Droning Sub-Task.
