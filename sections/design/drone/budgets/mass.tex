The mass budget in \autoref{tab:drone_mass_budget} summarizes the estimated mass 
of all Drone components. The estimated system subtotal is \qty{2.791}{\kilogram}. 
A 15\% engineering margin accounts for manufacturing tolerances, minor unlisted 
hardware, and possible integration changes, resulting in a calculated 
Maximum Take-Off Mass (MTOM) of \qty{3.210}{\kilogram}. This remains below the 
\qty{5.0}{\kilogram} limit for the Droning Sub-Task.
