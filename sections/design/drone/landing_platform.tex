To execute the additional task within the Droning Sub-Task, the rover
features a modular mobile landing platform with a radius of
\qty{0.4}{\meter}. Fabricated from aluminium alloy to ensure stability
during autonomous flight operations, the structure securely mounts to the
upper chassis for rapid attachment and removal.
