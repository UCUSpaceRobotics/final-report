The Drone communication system utilizes a multi-band radio frequency (RF)
architecture (\autoref{tab:drone_rf_links}) comprising a
\qty{2.4}{\giga\hertz} radio control (RC) link, a \qty{5.8}{\giga\hertz}
analog video link, and a \qty{433}{\mega\hertz} telemetry link. Full
parameters are provided in the Final RF Form, submitted as a separate
document.

\begin{table}[!htbp]
    \centering
    \caption{Drone radio frequency (RF) link allocation}
    \label{tab:drone_rf_links}
    \begin{tabular}{C{0.6cm} L{2.2cm} L{4cm} L{4.5cm}}
        \toprule
        \textbf{\#} & \textbf{Link} & \textbf{Band} & \textbf{Hardware} \\
        \midrule
        1 & RC Link        & \qty{2.4}{\giga\hertz}             & RC transmitter \& receiver \\
        2 & Video Link      & \qtyrange{5725}{5875}{\mega\hertz} & Video transmitter \& receiver \\
        3 & Telemetry Link  & \qty{433}{\mega\hertz}             & Telemetry transceivers \\
        \bottomrule
    \end{tabular}
\end{table}
