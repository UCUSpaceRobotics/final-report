The flight controller runs ArduPilot, which provides the required flight-control 
and failsafe functions. If the communication link is lost or a critical battery 
condition is detected, the Drone aborts the mission and initiates an emergency landing. 
A software geofence limits the permitted flight volume and prevents the Drone from 
leaving the operational area. The RC transmitter also provides manual override switches, 
allowing the operator to interrupt autonomous flight, regain manual control, and change flight modes.
